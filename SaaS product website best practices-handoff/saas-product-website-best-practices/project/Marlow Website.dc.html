How it works

From store URL to send-ready email in three steps.

Connect once. After that, every campaign starts from a single prompt.

01

Connect your brand

Paste your store URL. Marlow pulls your logo, colors, fonts, and products — automatically. One time, then it's saved.

02

Describe the email

One line about the campaign and a type — promo, launch, holiday, or newsletter. Pick a product to feature if you like.

03

Review & export

Tweak any section in place, then copy clean HTML straight into your ESP. Subject, preview text, and design — all done.
