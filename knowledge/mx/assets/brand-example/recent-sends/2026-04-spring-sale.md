**Send date:** 2026-04-08
**Type:** Promotional campaign
**Segment:** Full list (subscribers excluded from discount, received a variant)

## Subject and preview

Subject: "Spring coffees are here - free shipping on $40+"
Preview: "Three new lots just landed. The Huila is back."

## Summary

Announced the arrival of the spring single-origin rotation (Huila Colombia,
Ethiopia Yirgacheffe, and a new Oaxaca Mexico lot). Highlighted the Huila as
a returning seasonal favorite. Reinforced the free-shipping threshold as the
primary call to action. No discount code was used. Subscriber variant noted the
new arrivals and reminded them of their 10% add-on discount instead.

## Performance notes

Above-average open rate for a promotional send. Click rate was highest on the
Huila product link, consistent with it being the most recognized lot in the
lineup. Oaxaca underperformed in clicks; may benefit from more origin context
in future sends.
