## Entry 4: Morning Standard formula

The Morning Standard blend formula has not changed since the first roast in
2022. Mara considers it the anchor of the lineup and has declined requests to
release a medium-roast or pour-over variant of it.

Source: spruce-coffee.example.com/blog/morning-standard-story (fictional,
brand blog, as of 2023-06-15)
Use in: Morning Standard product emails, brand heritage contexts.


## Entry 5: Founder background

Mara managed a coffee bar in NE Portland from 2015-2019. Left to work in
restaurant supply, then returned to coffee in 2022 to start Spruce.

Source: spruce-coffee.example.com/about (fictional, as of 2024-01-10)

## Entry 6: Shipping policy origin

Free shipping over $40 was introduced in March 2024 after customer survey
feedback that shipping cost was the top friction point for reorders. The
threshold was chosen to keep the starter kit and most multi-bag orders above it.

Source: internal notes, confirmed by Mara in brand onboarding session
2024-03-01 (fictional)
Use in: reminder copy about free shipping threshold, cart-abandonment contexts.


## Entry 7: Filter recommendation

Mara recommends Hario V60 filters for the single-origins and any pour-over
brewer for the blends. She avoids recommending specific grinders publicly
because "the gear conversation takes over and the coffee gets lost."

Source: spruce-coffee.example.com/brew-guide (fictional, as of 2025-05-01)
Use in: brewing guides, post-purchase educational flows.
