# Framework: Referral Ask

Referral Ask leverages real positive sentiment into customer referrals. Use it AFTER a customer has expressed appreciation: post-review send, post-5-star-rating, post-positive-DM, post-loyalty-milestone. Use it in standalone campaigns when the brand has a real referral program with real rewards. Do not use it cold; referral asks without a prior warm signal underperform. Do not use it as a generic "tell a friend" send. Playbook rule: referral works best AFTER real appreciation (e.g. after a positive review). The ask stays simple. Sharing should feel like a natural extension of affinity, not a transaction.

## Structural rules

- Opening: acknowledge the prior warm signal explicitly. "I saw your review come through" / "Your reply landed in my inbox" / "Thank you for being one of our top supporters."
- Why they came to mind: one short beat. Honest reason: they get it, they have people like them, the brand thought of them specifically.
- Simple referral offer or ask: explicit. The referral mechanic (give X, get Y) or a plain ask without a mechanic. Either works; do not stack both.
- CTA: one. Verb matches ("Share [BRAND]", "Send your link", "Pass it along"). Destination is the referral page or the share link.
- Sign-off: personal. Founder first name common; referral asks read stronger when signed.
- Do not stack a second CTA or sneak in a discount push.

## When the brief should call it

```
**Campaign Direction**: <one sentence naming the prior warm signal and the referral ask>

**Offer**: <exact referral mechanic if one exists, else "No incentive">

**Links**: <verified referral or share destination>

**Framework**: Referral Ask
```

The brief carries the prior warm signal and the referral mechanic. The copywriter does not invent a review the customer did not leave.

## Example structural fragment

```
Hey [FIRST_NAME],

<OPENING: acknowledge the prior warm signal explicitly>

<WHY THEY CAME TO MIND: one short honest beat>

<REFERRAL OFFER OR ASK: explicit mechanic or plain ask>

[<CTA VERB PHRASE>](<verified link from brief>)

<SIGN-OFF: founder first name>

[UNSUBSCRIBE_LINK]
```

If the email opens with "We are running a referral program!" without referencing the customer's prior signal, the framework has drifted into cold solicitation. Replace with the specific moment that earned the ask.
