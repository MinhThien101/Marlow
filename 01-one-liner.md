# Step 1 — The One-Liner

**Target user:** Solo ecom founder (small DTC brand, no marketing team, can't justify an agency)
**Wedge (the painful old way):** Hiring an agency or freelancer

---

## Primary one-liner

> **[Product] helps solo ecom founders write and design on-brand marketing emails in minutes — without hiring an agency.**

Passes the test:
- **Specific person:** solo founder, no team
- **Specific outcome:** write *and* design a finished email (the two AI pillars)
- **Painful old way:** agency retainers + slow turnaround

---

## Variants (pick by what you lead with)

- **Speed-led:** ...go from idea to a finished, on-brand email in minutes — without hiring an agency.
- **Cost-led:** ...launch agency-quality email campaigns for a fraction of the cost — without hiring anyone.
- **Confidence-led:** ...who can't write or design send emails that look like a pro made them — without hiring an agency.

---

## Watch-out for Step 2+

"Faster + cheaper than an agency" is a *positioning* claim, not a *product* claim.
The founder's real fear isn't cost — it's shipping something that looks amateur and not knowing it.
The product must deliver **"looks pro"** or the one-liner overpromises.

Everything downstream (landing copy, core flow, the gate) inherits from this sentence.
